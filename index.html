About Us

Confidentive LLC provides personalized, at-home technical and educational support to senior citizens, helping them navigate digital devices and online tools with confidence. Our goal is to make technology accessible, simple, and enjoyable for every senior we serve.

Our Services

  • One-on-one technology assistance at home
  • Training on using smartphones, computers, and tablets
  • Guidance on staying safe online
  • Support with setting up and managing digital accounts and devices
